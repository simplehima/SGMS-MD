Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Thank you for using SGMS (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect your email address and name during registration. Authentication is securely handled through Firebase Authentication. Passwords are never stored in plain text.
  • Usage and Log Data: We collect technical data such as crash logs, device type, app version, and timestamps for the purpose of improving performance and reliability.
  • Optional Data: When you use collaborative features (e.g., shared projects or course progress), we may temporarily store related operational data for functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and manage user access
  • To provide, maintain, and improve app functionality
  • To diagnose crashes and enhance stability
  • To notify users about critical updates

3. Data Storage and Security

All user data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard security via Google Firebase. Sensitive operations are protected through secure key management and controlled update mechanisms.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data is only shared in limited cases with:

  • Service Providers: Firebase Authentication and Crashlytics process data under strict privacy terms.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court order.

5. Your Data Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your data. To request deletion, please visit our Account Deletion Page or email hima.azab.eg@gmail.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as your SGMS account is active or as necessary to provide services. Crash logs and analytics data are automatically purged periodically.

7. Third-Party Services

SGMS integrates with:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Google Play Services

8. Children’s Privacy

SGMS is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If such data is discovered, we will promptly delete it.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Updates will appear on this page with a new “Last updated” date. Significant changes may also be announced within the app.
