SGMS — Account Deletion Request

Use this page to request permanent deletion of your SGMS account and associated data.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email from the same email address linked to your SGMS account to:
    hima.azab.eg@gmail.com (preferred).
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My SGMS Account.
  3. Include in the email:
    • Your registered SGMS email address
    • Optional: a short note of what you want removed (e.g., projects, chat messages)

What will be deleted

  • Account record (email, name, user ID)
  • Project and course data owned by your account
  • Chat messages you authored within shared projects
  • Associated license keys issued to your account

What may be retained briefly

For security, auditing, and operational reasons we may retain limited data for short periods:

  • Crash and diagnostic logs: up to 30 days
  • Backup snapshots (to allow safe rollback): up to 14 days

Processing time

Once we verify your request, we will permanently remove your account and associated data within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete.

If you need assistance

If you have any questions or cannot use the registered email, contact: hima.azab.eg@gmail.com.
